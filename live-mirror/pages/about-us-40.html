5 Things Homeowners
Should Know Before Repairing Their Roof

  • Act Quickly After Storms: Address roof damage right away to avoid further issues.
  • Get a Professional Inspection: Ensure all damage, visible or hidden, is assessed.
  • Get It Documented: A professional inspection gives you dated photos and a written report - you keep the records and decide whether to involve your insurer.
  • Choose Quality Materials: Opt for durable, weather-resistant materials for lasting protection
  • Hire a Licensed Contractor: Ensure you work with a licensed, insured team for reliable service.
