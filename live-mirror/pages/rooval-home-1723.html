⨳ FAQ ⨳

Let’s climb through your questions, one by one.

How do I know if my roof needs to be replaced?

If your roof is over 15–20 years old, has missing shingles, leaks, or visible sagging, it may be time for a replacement. Our experts can inspect your roof and recommend whether repair or replacement is the best solution.

How long does a roof replacement take?

Most residential roof replacements are completed within 1–3 days, depending on roof size, weather conditions, and material type. We always aim to minimize disruption to your home.

What types of roofing materials do you offer?

We install a variety of roofing materials, including asphalt shingles, metal roofing, tile, and flat roofing systems — all sourced from trusted manufacturers for long-lasting performance.

Do you inspect for storm, wind, and hail damage?

Yes. We’ll inspect your roof and document any storm, wind, or hail damage we find, with clear photos and a written report. You keep that documentation to use however you choose. We walk you through the claims process, and give you an honest, thorough record of your roof’s condition.

Do you offer free roof inspections or estimates?

Yes, we offer free, no-obligation roof inspections and quotes. Our team will assess your roof’s condition and provide a detailed estimate before any work begins.
