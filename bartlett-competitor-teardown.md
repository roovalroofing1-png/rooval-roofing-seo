# Competitor teardown — Bartlett Roofing (bartlettroofs.com)

Analyzed 2026-07-23 at owner's request ("one of our competitors — see what they're doing and what works well"). Bartlett is a **direct Utah competitor** (they run a Salt Lake City service-area page) but a much bigger, multi-state operation: HQ Boise ID, serving UT/ID/WA/GA, 25+ years, founded early '90s by Doug Bartlett (family framing crew → roofing). 4.6★ / 430+ Google reviews.

## ✅ What works well (ADOPT — compliant with Rooval's rules)

1. **Trust-badge row, front and center (their #1 move).** Directly under the hero headline: **Owens Corning Platinum Preferred Contractor**, BBB A+, Inc. 500, Top 100 Roofing Contractors. Certifications are their lead trust signal and are repeated on About + local pages. **This is the single biggest thing Rooval should copy** — and it ties straight into the owner's "we use Owens Corning" note. If Rooval is an OC-certified/Preferred contractor, badge it prominently; pair with DOPL license + 5-star Google + BBB (if accredited) + "family-owned Utah."
2. **A specific experience anchor, repeated.** "25+ years of experience" appears in the hero, About, services, and local pages. A concrete, repeated credibility number (years, or "family-owned since ____") builds trust fast. Rooval needs its own number.
3. **Founder / origin story.** About page: "What started as a small family-run framing crew back in the early 90s has grown into one of the most trusted roofing companies…" — humanizes the brand. Rooval is family-owned (owner confirmed) → a short founder story is easy trust.
4. **Brand values with a voice.** "The Bartlett Method": Family First, **Radical Honesty ("we don't hide behind fine print or sugarcoat the truth")**, Extreme Ownership, Golden Rule. This honest, anti-hard-sell voice matches exactly what Rooval's owner keeps asking for. A short "How we work / our promise" block (honest inspection, no hard sell, straight answers) fits Rooval perfectly.
5. **Hero lead-capture form.** "Complimentary Roof Inspection" — a short form (name / phone / address / project type) sits IN the hero, not just a button. Captures the lead immediately.
6. **Real project gallery + specific testimonials.** /gallery/ of real jobs; testimonials are *specific* (seam checks, detailed line-item proposals, transparency) — far more convincing than a bare star rating. Rooval has real CompanyCam photos + strong Google reviews to feature.
7. **Clean service-silo structure.** residential-roofing/ → replacement, repair, asphalt, metal, low-slope. Good for SEO topical coverage.
8. **Warranty explainer.** A page that clearly distinguishes workmanship vs. manufacturer warranty and explains Owens Corning's coverage tiers. (Adopt the *clarity*, NOT the specific term — see below.)

## ⛔ What NOT to copy (violates Rooval's owner rules / UT law)

1. **Insurance-claims positioning.** Bartlett runs "Insurance Claims Assistance," "help you navigate the claims process," and FAQ items like "what if my claim gets denied?" / "your rates won't go up." → Directly against Rooval's insurance rule ([[insurance-messaging-rule]]): stay OUT of insurance. Do NOT adopt.
2. **"Roof giveaway."** A testimonial references a Bartlett "roof giveaway." A free-roof giveaway is exactly the "free roof" territory that's illegal in Utah and off-limits for Rooval. Do NOT copy.
3. **Stating a specific warranty term.** Bartlett headlines "100% Workmanship Warranty for 5 Years." Rooval's rule: never state a specific term — say "workmanship warranty" only. Adopt their *clarity*, not the number.
4. **Review COUNT in copy.** Bartlett shows "430+ reviews." Rooval rule: say "5-star rated," never a count (goes stale). 
5. **Instant Roof Quote tool.** Bartlett has a sticky "Instant Roof Quote." Rooval *deliberately removed* its quote tool (owner directive) — don't re-add unless the owner reverses.
6. **Commercial roofing.** Bartlett is heavy commercial; Rooval is residential-focused — only relevant if the owner wants to expand.

## Prioritized recommendations for Rooval
1. **Homepage trust-badge row** (Owens Corning + DOPL licensed + 5-star Google + BBB if accredited + family-owned Utah) — highest impact, ties to the OC message.
2. **"Our promise / how we work" honesty block** (matches owner's voice; anti-hard-sell).
3. **Founder / family-owned story** on /about-us/.
4. **Real Utah project gallery** (CompanyCam photos, no reuse) + feature 2-3 specific Google reviews.
5. (Optional) Hero inspection-request form; warranty explainer (no term); tighter service silos.

- **Years in business / "family-owned since ___"** number.
